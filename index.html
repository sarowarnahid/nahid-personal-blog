About Nahid

This is Nahid Sarowar, Lecturer (Computer Operation), Bangabandhu Gov't Comeerce & Technical College. I completed my Bachelor's degree (CSE) from the World University of Bangladesh and Diploma of Computer Engineering from Rajshahi Polytechnic Institute.

My Home land is stands bank of the river Atrai. This is situated Singra Upazila,under the district of Natore, and under the Rajshahi division.

Around 5+ years experienced in IT Sectors when I worked three companies. IT service management, maintenance, IT Support, troubleshooting, was the main part of my job life.

Since 3 years now I'm working as a Front-end developer at Nourish Poultry & Hatchery Ltd. ERP Software development, ERP project support, Data manipulation & Data processing is the main role of this job.
